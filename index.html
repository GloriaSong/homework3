What's wrong with the picture?

The pink line which represents the number of cancer screening and prevention services indicates a sharp decrease of the trend. Meanwhile, the red arrow shows a upward trend of the abortion numbers.

If we do not give a close look, we may intuitively believe that since 2010, the number of abortions has overnumbered the number of cancer screening and prevention services. However, by looking at the data labels (the actual numbers), we will realized that the real number of cancer screening and prevention services should be far more than that of the abortion.

Thus, without a clear Y-Axis as reference, the picture gives the reader a misconception of the acutal situation regarding the two operations. It would be better to use a dual-axis chart to show the different scales.
